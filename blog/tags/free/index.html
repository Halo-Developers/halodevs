Best FREE Online Courses with Certificates

March 24, 2022 · 6 min read
Ahmed Bargady
Data Science Student & FullStack Web Developer

Where and How to Get Courses by field experts with Certificates for FREE?

From my experience, I have found that the best way to get courses is to get them by field experts with certificates, and the most platforms that offer this service for me are:

COURSERA and COGNITIVE CLASSES

COURSERA​

COURSERA is a free learning site that offers MOOCs (Massive Open Online Courses) courses from well-known universities. All COURSERA courses contain pre-recorded video lectures that you can watch when it is convenient for you.

important

Most COURSERA courses are paid in the first look, but you can ask for financial support to get a free courses with their certificates as much as you want.

Check How To Get Financial Support section below.

Coursera has programs together with universities that allow you to get a master degree or specializations. You can explore various college courses without any hassle.

This site is offering free programs from accredited institutions. These free certification courses online available on this platform are designed by a leading university.

Top Online Courses​

  • Computer Science: Java, C++, Javascript, Blockchain, Linux, Data Science, IOT, etc.
  • Information Technology: CyberSecurity, AWS, Google Cloud, SAP, and more.
  • Science and Engineering: Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemistry, Physics and Astronomy, and more.
  • Business: Leadership and Management, Finance, Marketing, Entrepreneurship, and more.
  • Social Sciences: Economics, Education, Governance and Society, Law, etc.
  • Language Learning: Learning English, Chinese, Korean, etc.

How To Get Financial Support​

Let's see a simple example, I want to get a free courses with certificates from COURSERA about Statistics. I searched for the course and then I found this one by Stanford University:

Introduction to Statistics

Registration is free, but later you need to pay to keep going with it. Also you can see there is a Aide financière disponible button, which you can click to get a financial support.

Let's fill this first form:

For the Question Pourquoi déposez-vous une demande d'aide financière?, just copy and past this answer:

I’m a student from Morocco and want to learn Data Science. I think it will be beneficial for my work. But I’ve no job of my own to carry the expenses to pay for the certificate of this course. I live only for my scholarship, it is very much difficult for me to gather such an amount of money for the certificate. Financial Aid will help me take this course without any adverse impact on my monthly essential needs. So I’m badly in need of this financial aid. I want to take this course as I want to learn. This course will boost my job prospects after graduation from my institute. It will help me perform better in understanding and learning this technology and give me an edge over my competitors. A verified certificate will attach credibility to the certificate I receive from this course. I plan to complete all assignments on or before time as I have done in previous Signature Track Courses. Also, I intend to participate in Discussion Forums, which I have found to supplement my learning immensely in the other online courses I have taken on Coursera. I also plan to grade assignments that are peer-reviewed which I believe will be an invaluable learning opportunity.

Then check the checkbox J'ai vérifié que mes informations sont à jour and click Suivant:

In the next page, set the first input to 0.

Then for the question : De quelle façon ce cours vous aidera-t-il à atteindre vos objectifs?, just copy and past this answer:

It is no doubt that Machine Learning opens up lots of opportunities for one’s career. The field took off in the 2010s and has been growing ever since. ML experts and Data Scientists are some of the most well-paid jobs in the world. I plan to use this course to improve my knowledge about the fundamentals of Machine Learning and Data Science. I plan to eventually specialize in Deep Learning after having learned and practiced the basics well enough. I read that this course also includes several practice assignments and a project, doing which would be immensely beneficial to the improvement of my practical skills in this area. Completing this course would also provide me the confidence to dive deep into data science and take on further challenging assignments. All of this would greatly help me in achieving my career goals in the future and also help me in eventually getting a data science job.

Then check the checkboxes like below and click Examiner:

In the last page click Soumettre votre demande to submit your application:

Congratulations! 🎉 You have successfully completed the application. Now after exatly 15 days you will receive a confirmation email to enter your entire course for free with its certificate.

COGNITIVE CLASSES​

Congnitive Classes AI cognitiveclass.ai is an online platform that offers courses in various fields of AI. Their technologies and content are powered by IBM.

It primarily provides courses in English but does have some courses in Japanese, Spanish and Russian.

Their courses treats: leading-edge technologies Blockchain, Data Science, AI, Cloud, Serverless, Docker, Kubernetes and more...

What you will get?​

After each course or each learning path, you will get a certificate from IBM and a badge that you can share on your social media networks.

IBM Recommended Learning Paths​

  • Big Data Fundamentals
  • Data Science Fundamentals
  • Containers, K8s and Istio on IBM Cloud
  • Data Science with Python
  • Blockchain for Developers
  • Reactive Architecture Foundations
tip

All the courses are free to take on Cognitive Class.

Example​

  1. Register or Sign in into the platform Sign In Register

  2. Let's check the courses tab and search for python as an example:

  1. Let's start with the easiest one: Python for Data Science

  1. Enroll the course and start learning

  2. Each time you complete a module or lesson you will have a short quiz to check your knowledge (3 to 5 questions).

  3. When you finish the entire course, you will have a final exam in 1 hour to be submited (Most of times, you can finish the final exam in less than 15 minutes because it still simple)

  4. After completing your exam with success, you will get a badge and a certficates directly for free.

important

To pass the course you neet to get a score of 60% (some times it can be 70% ... it depends on the course) or higher in the final score (exam + quiz).

Check the progress tab to know to minimum score required to pass the course.
